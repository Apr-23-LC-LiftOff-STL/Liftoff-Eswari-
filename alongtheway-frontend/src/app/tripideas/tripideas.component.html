Road Trip Ideas Page

Having trouble coming up with your next big trip? Here are some suggestions we have for you!

Route 66

Estimated duration: 14 days

Distance: 2,448 miles

Description: Route 66, one of the most famous roads in the United States, spans over 2,400 miles and crosses 8 states, starting from Chicago, Illinois and ending in Santa Monica, California. You'll encounter a diverse palette of American landscapes and iconic roadside attractions along the way.

Key Highlights: Chicago, St. Louis, The Grand Canyon, Old Town Albuquerque, Santa Monica Pier

Pacific Coast Highway

Estimated duration: 5 days

Distance: 655 miles

Description: The Pacific Coast Highway (Highway 1) offers the quintessential California experience. The route spans most of the Pacific coastline of California and offers stunning views of the ocean, cliffs, and redwood forests.

Key Highlights: Monterey Bay Aquarium, Big Sur, Hearst Castle, Malibu, Santa Monica

Great Smoky Mountains National Park

Estimated duration: 3 days

Distance: Variable

Description: Explore America's most visited national park! With over 522,000 acres spanning North Carolina and Tennessee, there's plenty of room for exploration and enjoyment of the great outdoors.

Key Highlights: Clingmans Dome, Cades Cove, Roaring Fork, Mount LeConte

Yellowstone National Park

Estimated duration: 4 days

Distance: Variable

Description: Yellowstone, the first national park in the U.S. and widely held to be the first national park in the world, is known for its wildlife and its many geothermal features.

Key Highlights: Old Faithful, Yellowstone Lake, Grand Prismatic Spring, Hayden Valley

Blue Ridge Parkway

Estimated duration: 5 days

Distance: 469 miles

Description: Known as "America's Favorite Drive," this scenic route winds through the Appalachian Highlands and offers breathtaking views of the Blue Ridge Mountains.

Key Highlights: Humpback Rocks, Peaks of Otter, Mabry Mill, Linville Falls

Utah's Mighty 5 National Parks

Estimated duration: 7 days

Distance: Approximately 400 miles

Description: Experience the natural beauty of the American Southwest by visiting Utah's five incredible national parks: Zion, Bryce Canyon, Capitol Reef, Canyonlands, and Arches.

Key Highlights: The Narrows (Zion), Bryce Point (Bryce Canyon), Hickman Bridge (Capitol Reef), Mesa Arch (Canyonlands), Delicate Arch (Arches)

Overseas Highway (Florida Keys)

Estimated duration: 1 day

Distance: 113 miles

Description: The Overseas Highway, also known as U.S. Route 1, traverses the Florida Keys and includes 42 bridges over the Atlantic Ocean and Gulf of Mexico, providing incredible views.

Key Highlights: John Pennekamp Coral Reef State Park, Seven Mile Bridge, Bahia Honda State Park, Southernmost Point, Mallory Square
